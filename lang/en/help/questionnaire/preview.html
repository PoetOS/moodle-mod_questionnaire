Preview Questionnaire…

The Preview Questionnaire… button opens a popup window which displays a preview of your questionnaire. Your questionnaire will be displayed with the currently selected Questionnaire Theme.

If your questionnaire contains one or more Section Breaks, the Preview window will display all sections (called pages) on one single screen:, each section will be marked e.g. Page 1 of 3, Page 2 of 3, etc. at the top and at the bottom.
